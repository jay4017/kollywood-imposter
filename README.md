# kollywood-imposter
🎭 Project: Kollywood Imposter
A social deduction "pass-and-play" game designed for the Tamil community.

Inspired by the viral Futbol Impostor, this web app bridges the gap between traditional family charades and modern party games. Built specifically for Kollywood fans, it challenges players to blend in using their knowledge of Tamil cinema history (2000–2025), iconic stars, and legendary technicians.

🚀 Key Features
Offline "Pass-and-Play" Mechanic: Designed for physical gatherings. Use a single device for up to 15 players—no individual app downloads required.

Massive Content Library: Over 500+ hand-curated entries across two primary modes: Movies and Artists (Actors, Actresses, Directors, and Music Directors).

Tiered Difficulty: Data-driven gameplay with three distinct levels:

Easy (50%): Household names and mega-blockbusters (Rajini, Vijay, Ghilli).

Medium (35%): Cult hits and well-known supporting stars (Vada Chennai, Sai Pallavi).

Hard (15%): The "Geek Tier" featuring acclaimed technicians and indie gems.

Cinema-First UI: A high-contrast, mobile-responsive interface optimized for quick taps and group visibility during high-energy debates.

Imposter Scaling: Support for 1 to 3 imposters with randomized role assignment to keep every round unpredictable.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Frontend: React.js / Next.js

Styling: Tailwind CSS (Modern Cinema Aesthetic)

State Management: React Hooks (for player tracking and role randomization)

Deployment: Vercel

Data Structure: JSON-based local database for zero-latency performance.

🎮 How to Play
Setup: Enter the number of players and select your desired mode and difficulty.

The Reveal: Pass the phone around. Civilians see a secret Kollywood word; the Imposter(s) are told to "Blend In."

The Clue: Everyone gives a one-word hint or a signature 5-second charade action.

The Accusation: Debate, scream, and point fingers until the group decides who the "vetti scene" imposter is!

The Reveal: Hit the Reveal button to catch the imposter or realize you've been fooled.
